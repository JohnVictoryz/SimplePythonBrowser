Facts & Questions

Do you provide support?

Yes, we do provide help on our Discord Server!

Is this safe?

Yes, it is secure. Some antivirus programs may report that it is a virus, although it is not.

How to get future updates?

You may receive the latest updates from our Github or by going to Help/Update in the browser.

how is your browser different?

Big companies may claim that their product is the best, but we are honest: ours is not the best by any means, but it was created with love by a small indie team, and we will try to support it for as long as possible, and we will do it for free, which is why it is open source and free. We are as transparent as possible.

how do i support you?

You can support us by visiting Buymeacoffee.com 50% of revenues are donated to charity; the program is and will remain free, so you are not required to pay.

why did you start this project?

Because existing browsers do not suit us, we created our own.
